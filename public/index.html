What We Do

NannyNow was designed to help you find care service providers in your area for you or your loved ones, anytime, anywhere. Think of us as a middle ground, connecting you with capable, pre-qualified care providers in your area. Yes we know, it's that simple.

How We Do

Because we value your security, all of our care providers are vetted before becoming available for service through NannyNow.Certifications are in some cases necessary based on the type of service provided. Our thorough qualifying process is done with the goal to facilitate your search experience, giving you freedom to choose and peace of mind.

Why We Do

NannyNow was created with the goal to connect people in your community, bringing your friends and neighboors together with the intention of caring for oneanother. NannyNow seeks new ways to extend your community, building bonds, improving our lives, and our neighborhoods.

Becoming a care provider



  • As a care provider

    Having an account as a care provider with NannyNow is limitless, you are now able to offer your care services and accept jobs sent to you by users in your area!

  • Becoming a provider

    As an approved service provider you are now able to create an account which will be your calling card. Users seeking care service will be able to search for service providers in their areas. So be sure to use clear photos, and share a few details about yourself that would be of importance to those looking for care service.

  • Qualify to become a provider

    Once an applicant has been verified and we can ensure they meet our required safety standards, an applicant is sent a copy of their federal background check and is now free to create a service provider account.

  • Apply to become a provider

    The first step in the process of providing care service through NannyNow requires all of our applicants to submit their information to be filtered through our vetting process. This filtering process allows us to ensure and maintain a pool of qualified service providers at your service.
